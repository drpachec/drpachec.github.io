About

Dan Pacheco is a Professor of Practice and holds the Peter A. Horvitz Chair of Journalism Innovation at the Newhouse School and is a pioneer in the use of virtual reality for journalism. In 2014 he co-produced The Des Moines Register's Harvest of Change VR project. Harvest of Change earned an Edward R. Murrow Award in 2015 for innovation in 360-degree video.

Previously, Pacheco spent 20 years in digital publishing at Fortune 500 companies and startups. He started his career as an online producer for Washingtonpost.com and later served as principal product manager at AOL.
